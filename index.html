Frequently Asked Questions

Is 4all-Downloader free to use? +

Yes, 4all-Downloader is completely free with no hidden payments or subscription fees. It's also ad-free and provides transparent pricing with active user support.

What languages does 4all-Downloader support? +

We support 10+ languages including English, French, Japanese, Korean, German, Spanish, Hindi, Chinese, and 2 additional languages. Note that Arabic is not currently supported.

Can I use my own download engines? +

Yes! Besides built-in engines (yt-dlp, aria2c, curl, mix2), you can integrate custom engines by providing format mapping. Check our documentation for detailed setup instructions.

Does it have a built-in web browser? +

Yes! 4all-Downloader includes a full-featured web browser with JavaScript injection support for content detection, analysis, ad-blocking, and direct downloading from any website.

Can I convert files without quality loss? +

Absolutely! Our professional-grade lossless converter supports MP4, MP3, MKV, AVI, WAV, and many other formats with quality selection (high/medium/low) and batch processing capabilities.

Does it remember my download history? +

Yes, 4all-Downloader tracks your download history for easy reference and re-downloading. You can also export your download lists, history, and settings for backup or sharing across devices.

Can I use direct yt-dlp commands? +

Yes! We provide a command panel where you can paste and execute direct yt-dlp commands, giving power users complete control over their downloads with custom parameters.

What about privacy and data protection? +

Privacy is protected with no data retention policy. 4all-Downloader doesn't remember or track your browsing activity, ensuring your privacy while providing full functionality.

Can I download torrents and other file types? +

Yes! Beyond video/audio, 4all-Downloader supports torrents, documents, images, ZIP files, ISO files, and other standard file types for comprehensive downloading needs.

Does it automatically add audio to video files? +

Yes! Our intelligent system analyzes video formats (AVC, Opus, etc.) and automatically adds audio tracks to video-only formats. You can also choose to download without audio if preferred.

Can I use proxy servers and custom network settings? +

Absolutely! 4all-Downloader supports both SOCKS5 and HTTP proxies, custom user agents, browser cookie integration, and configurable download threads for optimal performance.

Is it legal to download videos? +

It depends on the content and your local laws. Please ensure you have permission to download content and comply with copyright laws and platform terms of service.
